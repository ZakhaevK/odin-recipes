Tuna and Cheese Jaffle

Description

Warning: Requires a Jaffle-maker.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Tuna
  • Cheese
  • Lemon
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Open a can of tuna.
  2. Lay out the tuna on bread.
  3. Apply pepper to the tuna.
  4. Squeeze the lemon on the tuna as per your taste.
  5. Place cheese on top.
  6. Place another slice of bread to make it a sandwich.
  7. Cook in your jaffle-maker and enjoy.
