Burrito

Description

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Mincemeat
  • El Paso Meat Spices
  • Onion
  • Habanero Sauce
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Dice the tomato and onion.
  2. Lay out the wraps
  3. Place lettuce on the wraps horizontally.
  4. Place tomato near the lettuce, and apply pepper.
  5. Place cheese nearby the tomato, and apply pepper.
  6. Place onion where the meat will sit in the wrap.
  7. Place mincemeat in a pan and cook until brown.
  8. Apply Meat Mix to the mincemeat and reduce heat to let it absorb into it.
  9. Place egg in a pan and scramble it until ready.
  10. Place meat on the cheese in the wrap, and apply habanero sauce to it.
  11. Place egg on top of the meat.
  12. Wrap the burrito/s and enjoy.
